  • Phishing Website: Spear Phishing Project

    In this project, I took the role of an attacker to gain a deeper understanding of their thought processes. The goal was to exploit GitHub by executing a spear phishing attack. To accomplish this, I created a convincing phishing website that closely resembled GitHub's official site. Crucially, I ensured that victims, upon visiting the phishing site, were seamlessly redirected to the legitimate site, leaving no trace or suspicion. This project provided valuable insights into the tactics and techniques employed by attackers in compromising user login information.
